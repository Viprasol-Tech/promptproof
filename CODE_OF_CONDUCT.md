# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for
everyone, and to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviour includes empathy, respect for differing views, graceful
acceptance of constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the
community. Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory
comments, and publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainers
at [support@viprasol.com](mailto:support@viprasol.com). All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
